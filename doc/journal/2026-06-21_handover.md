## Problems / Blockers

- **Reduced chi² needs per-point CoG uncertainties**, which we do NOT currently
  store. The isophote `prof` tables have `intens_err`, but CoG (cumulative) errors
  must be derived/propagated, or use an estimated noise level. Resolve early in
  exp07 (otherwise AIC/BIC via RSS with an assumed Gaussian noise, and rely on
  residual structure + cross-validated error).
- Open data gaps (non-blocking): exact snap-72 `mass_halo` (M0 is snap-71 proxy);
  no `halo_id` cross-match to DiffMAH/Diffstar.

## Key Decisions

- **Adopt MAH-PCA** (top ~4 PCs of M0-normalized log-MAH) as the principled
  halo-side representation (equals hand-picked summaries, uses the full curve).
- **Single-component radial-DiffMAH** is the profile parameterization; bounded fit.
- **CoG (not Σ)** is the modeling target (more compressible/stable; same info).
- **Don't over-explain residual scatter** by secondary properties without evidence.
